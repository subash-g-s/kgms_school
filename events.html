Rules To Be Followed Strictly

Clear expectations for a disciplined school environment

1

Students must bring the school calendar daily.

2

Students must attend school before first bell and assembly without fail.

3

Uniform and identity card are compulsory.

4

Damaging school property is punishable.

5

Students are responsible for their belongings.

6

Girls should avoid costly jewellery.

7

Running and screaming in class or verandah is prohibited.

8

Students must greet teachers respectfully.

9

Waste materials should be disposed properly.

10

Leave letters must be signed by parents.

11

Habitual late coming may result in dismissal.

12

Complaints must be addressed only to the Principal.

Principal Meeting Time Every Friday, 3:30 PM to 4:30 PM
