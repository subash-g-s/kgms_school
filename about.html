Our Institution

Krishnaswamy Nagar - Ganga Nagar Matriculation School

Recognised by the Department of Education, Tamil Nadu, our school is an English medium institution that prepares students for the Samacheer Examination with equal attention to character, discipline, and a sense of service.

Run by the Amenities and Welfare Association of Krishnaswamy Nagar - Ganga Nagar, the school continues the founding vision of Sri N. Chinnaswamy Naidu by offering a nurturing environment where every child can grow in mind, conduct, and confidence.

School Motto

SMILE AND SERVE

"SMILE AND SERVE" is our School Motto.

  • To be brave and courageous always.
  • To face every odd experience in life with a smile.
  • To remain balanced in joy or sorrow.

The calmness of the mind expresses itself in an eternal smile.

School History

A timeline of meaningful progress

1971

The school was founded by Sri N. Chinnaswamy Naidu with the aim of giving children in the locality access to disciplined, value-rich education.

Early Years

The institution began with classes from LKG to V Standard, building a strong elementary foundation for young learners.

Growth and Transition

As the school expanded in trust and capability, it was later converted into a Matriculation School to serve students through higher levels.

Current Vision

Education without discrimination

Our school admits and nurtures children without discrimination based on caste, religion, or sex. We strive to create a learning climate where dignity and fairness are lived values.

The institution places equal importance on mental, moral, and physical development so that students mature into worthy citizens of the country.

Institutional Purpose

Balanced growth for every child

Academic performance is strengthened by discipline, prayerful reflection, healthy habits, and service-minded living. We believe the finest education equips students to think clearly, act responsibly, and contribute generously.

That combination of heritage, care, and aspiration continues to shape the KGMS identity today.
