Our Story

A school built on the strength of values

Krishnaswamy Nagar Ganga Nagar Matriculation School was founded with a simple aim — to make quality, value-based education accessible to families in and around Sowripalayam Pirivu, Coimbatore. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant learning community known for academic results, disciplined campus life and the warmth of our teachers.

Affiliated to the Tamil Nadu State Board, our school offers classes from the Kindergarten years all the way through matriculation, with a curriculum that balances core academics, life skills and cultural learning.
