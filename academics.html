Programmes

Stages of Learning

From the first steps of pre-primary to the rigour of matriculation — we shape every stage with care.

Pre-Primary

LKG & UKG • Ages 3–5. Play-based learning, phonics, early numeracy and storytelling.

Primary

Classes I–V. Building strong foundations in language, math, science and social studies.

Middle

Classes VI–VIII. Sharpening conceptual depth, critical thinking and project work.

Secondary

Classes IX–X. Focused board preparation, subject mentoring and personalised remedial care.

Languages

Tamil, English & Hindi taught with emphasis on reading, writing and confident expression.

Co-Curricular

Music, art, dance, sports and clubs that round off academic learning.

Our Approach

How we teach

  • Smart classrooms
    Visual, interactive teaching with audio-visual aids.
  • Continuous assessment
    Regular tests, quizzes and feedback to track every child.
  • Reading programme
    Daily reading time and a stocked school library.
  • Activity-based learning
    Hands-on projects, experiments and group work.
  • Remedial support
    Extra coaching for students who need a helping hand.
  • Parent-teacher partnership
    Regular meetings, transparent reports, open communication.
